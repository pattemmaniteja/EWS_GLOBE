Safety Rules & Unsafe Practices

      Remember that "SAFETY FIRST, WORK NEXT" .


      • Know your job and follow instructions.

      • Avoid wearing clothing that might catch, moving or rotating parts. Long sleeves of shirts, long hair, neck tie and jewellery are definite hazards in the shop.

      • Wear safety shoes. Do not wear canvas shoes; they give no resistance to hard objects dropped on the feet.

      • Keep the area around machine or work clean.

      • Be sure that all guards are in place.

      • One person only should operate the machine controls.

      • Use tools correctly and do not use them if they are not in proper working condition.

      • Wear safety goggles when working in areas, where sparks or chips of metal are flying.

      • Get to know who in-charge of first aid is and where boxes are placed and where the first aid can be found in case of emergency.
